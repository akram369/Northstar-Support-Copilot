# Roles and Permissions

## Built-in roles
Owners control billing, security settings, and organization deletion. Administrators manage members, integrations, and workspace configuration. Members can create and edit ordinary records. Viewers have read-only access. Only an Owner can assign or remove the Owner role.

## Permission troubleshooting
For an unexpected `403`, confirm the user is signed into the intended organization and workspace. Then compare the action with the effective role shown under Settings > Members. Role changes can take up to two minutes to propagate; sign out and back in after that window if the session still shows old permissions.

## Sensitive changes
Support cannot grant roles, transfer ownership, or bypass organization policy. Ownership disputes and changes involving a departed owner must be escalated for identity and authorization review.

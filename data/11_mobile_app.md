# Mobile Application Support

## Supported systems
The Northstar mobile app supports iOS 16 or later and Android 10 or later. If sign-in loops, update the app, verify automatic date and time, switch between Wi-Fi and cellular data, and retry. Reinstall only after confirming unsynced offline changes are not present.

## Synchronization
Changes made offline are queued until connectivity returns. A pending-sync badge indicates unsent changes. Keep the app open after reconnecting until the badge clears. If a conflict occurs, the app preserves the server version and places the offline copy in Conflict Review.

## Diagnostics
From Settings > Help > Diagnostics, users can copy a diagnostic ID that contains app version and non-sensitive synchronization events. The diagnostic package excludes record contents and credentials.

# Data Exports and Recovery

## Exports
Organization Owners and Administrators can request an export under Settings > Data. An export is prepared asynchronously and the requester receives a download link when ready. The link expires after 24 hours. Large organizations may require several hours; no fixed completion estimate is guaranteed.

## Deleted records
Ordinary deleted records remain in Trash for 30 days and can be restored by an Administrator. After 30 days they are permanently removed from the customer interface. Organization deletion starts a seven-day cancellation window and requires Owner confirmation.

## Recovery requests
Support cannot restore permanently deleted content from chat. Accidental deletion involving critical data must be escalated promptly with organization ID, record type, deletion time, and actor. Recovery is not guaranteed.

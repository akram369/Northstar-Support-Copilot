# Service Incidents and Status Communication

## Incident impact
The public status page is the authoritative source for active service incidents and affected regions. Support agents may describe only the impact and updates shown there. They must not invent restoration estimates. If no estimated recovery time is published, state that engineering is investigating and offer the incident subscription link.

## Customer actions
During a confirmed incident, avoid repeated configuration changes because they can complicate recovery. Capture the customer region, affected workflow, first observed time, request IDs, and approximate percentage of failed operations. Business-critical impact should be attached to the incident for prioritization.

## Post-incident reports
For severity-one incidents, Northstar publishes a customer-facing incident review within five business days. The report includes impact, timeline, contributing factors, corrective actions, and prevention work.
